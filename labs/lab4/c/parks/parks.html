Bushnell


Our first park on the list is Bushnell Park. It is located in Downtown Hartford.
On one end, the Capitol sits on a hill and overlooks the open space of the park.
It is expansive and has a carousel, too.


Riverside Park


This park lines the Connecticut River as it winds through Downtown Hartford.
As such, it is home to many of Hartford's public water activities.


Elizabeth Park


This is Hartford's most famous park because it contains the country's
first public rose garden. While the park's land is split between Hartford
and West Hartford, it is still maintained by the city of Hartford.
Elizabeth Park has a restaurant overlooking its pond and ice cream
in the summertime.
