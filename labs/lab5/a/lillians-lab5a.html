Grilled Cheese


Ingredients


  • Sourdough bread
  • Cheese, preferably cheddar and mozzarella
  • Butter (Irish)
  • Dijon mustard
  • Mayonnaise



You can substitute some of the ingredients, like type of bread and cheese. These are just
my favorite. For the mustard, this is a non-traditional addition, but it adds depth
and some tang to the sandwich. You can omit it or add mayo on the inside, too. Same with
the butter...you can put that inside or more on the outside, depending on your favorite flavors.
But, the butter should be the highest quality you can find.



Recipe


  1. Heat up pan on medium-low burner
  2. On one side of each slice of bread, spread a little mayonnaise
  3. On the other side, lightly spread Dijon mustard, but only on one slice
  4. Put the cheese on Dijon-spreaded slice
  5. Close the sandwich
  6. Place butter in pan. It must be sizzling.
  7. Place sandwich in pan. Leave and don't touch for about 5 minutes.
    Turn the heat up to medium
  8. Flip carefully and follow same instructions as number 7.
  9. Take out of pan, cut if desired, and enjoy!
