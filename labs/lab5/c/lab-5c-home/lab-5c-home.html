Welcome





Halloween has religious roots, but nowadays, it's a holiday that has a mind of its own.
Celebrities compete with each other and even their past-selves to adorn extravagant costumes;
costume selection is a big part of many kids' childhoods; pets even get to dress up, too.
There are also different ways to view the holiday. I like it because I love the dark, chilling
vibe of the fall, but others choose to view it more lighthearted. Either way, it is definitely
an important time of year, though I feel like we just left it.
